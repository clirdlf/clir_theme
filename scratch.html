DLF's groups help practitioners in our community work across institutional lines, and make the Digital Library Federation an effective and thoughtful agent of change. Here you will find what our more active DLF groups and affiliates are up to and how to get involved. If you want to start a new group or revive an older one, just let us know.

Get Involved

Want to get involved with a DLF Working Group, but don’t know where to start? Contact the group facilitator, join the e-mail list, and participate in an upcoming call!
